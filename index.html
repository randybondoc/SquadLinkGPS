Privacy Policy

App: SquadLink GPS
Effective Date: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the SquadLink GPS app ("the App") operated by Randy Bondoc. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy.

1. Information We Collect and Why

To provide the core safety and real-time mapping functionality of SquadLink GPS, we require access to certain data on your device:

Location Data (Including Background Location)
The App collects your real-time location data to plot your position on a shared map with your private squad members. This app collects location data to enable real-time squad tracking even when the app is closed or not in use. This is necessary so your squad can see your accurate location and distance during a ride.
  • Sensor Data (Accelerometer/Gyroscope): The App accesses your device's motion sensors to detect potential emergencies or crashes. If triggered, the app alerts your squad members.
  • User Profile Data: We collect your chosen username and display icon to identify you to your squad members on the map and in the squad list.

2. How We Share Your Data

Your location, username, and emergency status are only transmitted securely via our private server to the specific squad members in your actively joined private room. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with unauthorized third parties.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Location data is processed in real-time to facilitate the squad map and is not permanently stored on our servers after your session ends. You may request the deletion of any stored profile data by contacting us directly.
